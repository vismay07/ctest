FLOWERS

Aster

Why we love them: It's one of our favorite perennials for a reason! Aster blooms throughout the fall, adding a bright pop of summery color to your yard when all your other flowers aren't quite as visible.

Daylilies

These gorgeous flower are easy to divide, and you can build a collection of colors and shapes by simply adding a few plants year after year.

Lavender

Though lavender is practically indestructible and thoroughly deer-resistant, we're fans of its beauty and luscious scent.
